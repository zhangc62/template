At the end of this semester, I really learned and enjored this class, and of course I learned new things from each of the four projects.The most interesting and important project of this class I think it’s the formal research project, although it was the most hard essay to write in the entire semester. In my personal views, this project was designed to develop students’ self-learning skills and skills of use resources on campus. In this project, as a chemistry major student, I have to find one formal research article about chemistry fields and another informal article about chemistry. After that my job was to compare the differences between these two types of articles and point out why there are such huge difference and explain are these difference have some effect or influence to readers. More importantly, I have to use translate those formal words languages and concepts in chemistry fields to general and simple ways to people who are new to this area. When I look back to this project right now, I almost forget about the pain and difficulties of this project, but what I gained was the process of using campus resources and the understanding of building knowledge in different ways. Therefore, I would say the formal research project was the most interesting and meaningful project in this class.
