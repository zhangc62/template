Introduction

Nowadays, because of the growth economies and advanced technologies, we have various options of how to organize our life and make our life style become colorful and enjoyable. For instance, people could taste a different type of restaurants without traveling to particular countries. Because the variant monetary exchange rates, many international visitors would like to shop and buy plentiful items in other foreign counties. In the past, people were trying to survive from grim warfare; and people were starving for the lack of food. However, today our lives are getting better and better, most of the people live in the world don’t have to worry about food problems any more, because of the basic living are guaranteed, more people start to put their desires and interests on other places such as cars, fashion, food, and electronics. Therefore the word “consumer culture” finally came out. The word “consumer culture” refers to a theory that the entire human society is strongly influenced by consumerism. (This theory also states that economic and social cultures are based on the purchasing of commodities and services and that social functioning and behavior is bound up with the fostering a desire for these goods.)(The defection comes from http://dictionary.com/) Briefly, consumer culture is a strong desire of purchasing goods, and purchasing power is a main feature of the consumerism. The sneakerhead culture was one of the phenomenon of consumer culture, because of many commercials and celebrity effect, many people went crazy of buying sneakers, especially buying from Nike, and Air Jordan. More importantly, this sneaker cultural artifact not only became some social issues in the United States, but also related with environmental pollutions and some other horrible issues.
